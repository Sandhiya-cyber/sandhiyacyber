Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a profile?

To create a profile, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in the required details.

How can I search for matches?

You can search for matches by using the search filters available in your account dashboard.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, click on the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page and follow the instructions.

How can I contact customer support?

You can contact customer support by visiting the "Contact Us" page or by emailing support@matrimony.com.
